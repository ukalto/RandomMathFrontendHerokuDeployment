Game Description!

It is a simple game based on simple mathematics! 1 game includes 10 rounds with 20 seconds each.

After pushing the Play Game button a small counter displays and you will have to wait for 5 seconds, until the game starts.

A small timer is running beside the math operation. The math operation includes 2 random generated numbers between 1 and 999 and a random math sign: + | - | / | *

Right beside the math operation is an input field to put your solution in. You can wait until the timer is up or push the OK button if you think you are right and want to save time. Afterwards you get a message if your answer was wrong or right. Between each round you have 2 seconds to rest and prepare yourself for the next round. If you have managed to answer 10 math operations in a row you get your result. You have the possibility to check your overall score at your Profile. Furthermore, you have the option to climb on the Leaderboard to get the best in at least one categorie.
